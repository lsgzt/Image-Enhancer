Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the AI Image Enhancer.

What is an AI image enhancer?

An AI image enhancer is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to automatically improve photo quality. It can restore old, blurry, or low-resolution photos by sharpening details, upscaling resolution, and reconstructing faces using models like CodeFormer.

Is the AI Image Enhancer free to use?

Yes. The AI Image Enhancer is free to use online. There is no signup required — simply upload your image, choose your settings, and download the enhanced result.

What photo formats and sizes are supported?

You can upload JPG, PNG, and WebP images up to 10MB. The enhancer works best on photos that contain faces, but it also improves backgrounds, landscapes, and general image clarity.

How does face restoration work?

Face restoration uses a deep learning model called CodeFormer. It detects faces in your image, reconstructs missing or blurred facial details, and produces a sharper, more realistic version while preserving identity.

Can I upscale my photo without losing quality?

Yes. Choose an upscale factor between 1x and 4x to increase your photo's resolution. The AI adds realistic detail during the upscale so the final image stays sharp instead of looking pixelated.

Are my uploaded photos kept private?

Your photos are processed to generate the enhanced version and are not used to train any models. Always review the privacy policy of any online tool before uploading sensitive images.
